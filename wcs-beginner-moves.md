# West Coast Swing — 20 Beginner Moves for the Lead

A memorization reference for the first 20 lead patterns, ordered roughly the way they're taught:
foundations → 6‑count core → 6‑count with turns → 8‑count whips → beginner+ variations.

**How to read the footwork:** WCS is a slotted dance. The follower travels up and down the slot;
you (the lead) stay small, step to the side to open the slot, and rotate to face her. The default
building blocks are *walk, walk (1‑2) → triple step (3&4) → anchor (5&6)*. The **anchor** ends
almost every pattern — you stay put and stretch away from your partner.

> Conventions vary slightly by school. These use the common US convention of the lead starting on
> the **left foot**. Use the diagrams/notes as memory aids and the linked video as the authority
> for exact technique.

**Legend:** 🟢 = Core (must‑know) · 🟠 = Beginner+ (grow into these)

---

## Foundations

### 1. Starter Step  🟢
*Open basic / 4‑count rhythm break · 6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Facing partner in open position. 1 — rock back LEFT. 2 — replace forward RIGHT. 3&4 — triple in place (L‑R‑L). 5&6 — anchor (R‑L‑R).
- **Hands:** Hand‑to‑hand (your left holds her right), palm softly cupped, no grip. Elbow weighted slightly back for an elastic "stretch."
- **Cue:** Rock back, replace, triple, anchor — establish the pulse.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WHk1qylcQzQ · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-for-beginners/

### 2. Anchor Step  🟢
*The universal ending · 2 beats · 5&6*
- **Feet:** Triple in place (R‑L‑R), right foot back / left forward in a small stagger. Do **not** travel.
- **Hands:** Light leverage (leaning‑away) connection. Create tension by moving your center back, never by pulling the arm.
- **Cue:** Stay put and stretch back — never travel on the anchor.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mg9rgBxjCQg · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/category/anchor-step/

## 6‑Count Core

### 3. Sugar Push (Push Break)  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Stay at your end. 1 — back LEFT. 2 — back RIGHT (receive her). 3&4 — triple forward (L‑R‑L) sending her back. 5&6 — anchor.
- **Hands:** Two‑hand or hand‑to‑hand. On 3&4 give a soft "wall" (compression — meet, don't shove), then release so she rolls back out.
- **Cue:** Meet her with a soft wall, send her back home.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gYYYJNatVXQ · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-basic-patterns/

### 4. Left Side Pass  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** 1 — back LEFT, opening the slot. 2 — back/side RIGHT to clear the lane. 3&4 — triple as she passes on your **left**, rotating with her. 5&6 — anchor.
- **Hands:** Hand‑to‑hand. Lead her straight down the slot, hand in your centerline; let her pass on your left — don't drag her across.
- **Cue:** Open the door, let her pass left, rotate, anchor.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WwCDPCVcLMA · **Ref:** https://www.dancecentral.info/ballroom/club-dances/west-coast-swing/left-side-pass

### 5. Right Side Pass  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Mirror of the left side pass. 1 — back. 2 — clear the slot to your right. 3&4 — triple as she passes on your **right**. 5&6 — anchor.
- **Hands:** Hand‑to‑hand. Send her forward down the slot past your right side. Think "send," not "pull."
- **Cue:** Send her past your right side, rotate, anchor.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dfVpwMLqm-o · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-basic-patterns/

### 6. Underarm Turn (Right Side Pass w/ Underarm Turn)  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Same as a right side pass — stay small while she turns.
- **Hands:** On 2–3 raise your leading hand into a smooth arch above her head (no yanking). She passes on your right and turns **clockwise** under it; lower gently for the anchor.
- **Cue:** Raise a soft arch — she spins by on your right.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bSu7QJZ5SLU · **Ref:** https://www.howcast.com/videos/507298-how-to-do-the-underarm-turn-swing-dance/

### 7. Inside Roll (Left Side Pass w/ Inside Turn)  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Left‑side‑pass footwork, rotating with her.
- **Hands:** As she leaves on 1–2, lead a left (inside) turn: hand toward her shoulder line, then open, so she rolls toward you and turns **counter‑clockwise** on your left. Lead the body first, hand second.
- **Cue:** Roll her in on the left pass — turn is toward you.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=koIM1l49Aac · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/beginner-west-coast-swing-patterns/

### 8. Tuck Turn  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** 1 — back LEFT. 2 — small step as you tuck her. 3&4 — triple. 5&6 — anchor. Stay mostly in place.
- **Hands:** On 2 compress and "tuck" (a small check that turns her away), then lead her back down the slot and turn her under your raised hand on 5&6. Compress → redirect → release.
- **Cue:** Tuck (compress), send, turn out.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pqwm_flRXkk · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-basic-patterns/

### 9. Sugar Tuck  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Like a sugar push — stay home: back LEFT, back RIGHT, triple, anchor.
- **Hands:** Bring her in like a sugar push, but on 4 add a small tuck turning her away; she rolls back under your arm to face you by the end of 5&6.
- **Cue:** Sugar push, but tuck her away and roll her back.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UG75QnjhYVM · **Ref:** https://www.dancecentral.info/ballroom/club-dances/west-coast-swing/sugar-tuck

### 10. Passing Tuck (Traveling Tuck)  🟢
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Side‑pass footwork with travel: back, clear, triple as she tucks and passes, anchor at the new position.
- **Hands:** Add a tuck mid‑pass so she turns **while** traveling rather than staying at one end. Lead it early; don't stall her.
- **Cue:** A tuck she travels through — turn on the pass.
- **Video (playlist):** https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLeZ__vwnEDu94OYgg4g0llDcZnL2NodiL · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/beginner-west-coast-swing-patterns/

## 8‑Count Whips

### 11. Whip  🟢
*The signature 8‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5, 6, 7&8*
- **Feet:** Walk, walk, triple, walk, walk, triple. 1 — back LEFT. 2 — side/forward RIGHT bringing her in. 3&4 — triple swapping her across. 5,6 — two walks rotating around her. 7&8 — anchor.
- **Hands:** Left‑to‑right or two‑hand. Bring her toward a near‑closed position (right hand can catch her back), lead her past, then redirect ("whip") her back to her end over 5–6. Body leads, hand finishes.
- **Cue:** Bring her in, pass, whip her back — walk‑walk, triple ×2.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5vVeFs6q9os · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-whip/

### 12. Reverse Whip  🟠
*8‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5, 6, 7&8*
- **Feet:** Whip timing, but you rotate the opposite way (clockwise).
- **Hands:** Same catch as a whip, redirected the other direction. Keep the frame connected and lead the new direction with your body early.
- **Cue:** A whip that rotates the other way.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OLXJylMWZto · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/reverse-whip-series/

### 13. Whip with Inside Turn  🟠
*8‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5, 6, 7&8*
- **Feet:** Basic whip footwork.
- **Hands:** Around 5–6, raise your hand and lead an inside turn instead of a plain redirect; re‑catch for the anchor. Give her the full two walking beats.
- **Cue:** A whip with a spin baked into the middle.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eMUxDP0vOIA · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-whip/

### 14. Basket Whip (Locked Whip)  🟠
*8‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5, 6, 7&8*
- **Feet:** Whip timing; step slightly across to create the "basket" wrap, then unwind for the anchor.
- **Hands:** Both hands stay joined and wrap into a basket during the whip, then unwrap as she travels out. Keep tone so it doesn't collapse.
- **Cue:** Wrap into a basket, unwrap on the way out.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XrjWjMDHUTg · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-whip/

## Beginner+ Variations

### 15. Rolling Side Pass (Roll off the Arm)  🟠
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Side‑pass footwork.
- **Hands:** Lead a continuous roll (~1½ turns) so she "rolls off" your hand/arm to the far end. Keep the hand loose enough to let rotation continue.
- **Cue:** She rolls off your arm to the other end.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o10RKoM-1cE · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-basics-part-2/

### 16. Left Side Pass + Leader's Turn (Man's Turn)  🟠
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Left‑side‑pass footwork, but you add your own ½ turn on 3&4 (usually left), anchoring to face her again.
- **Hands:** Release just enough to turn yourself past the connection and re‑catch for the anchor. Rotate around a stable, quiet hand — don't tug her.
- **Cue:** She passes, YOU turn, re‑catch, anchor.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qrO1c9c8tNE · **Ref:** https://www.dancecentral.info/ballroom/club-dances/west-coast-swing/left-side-pass

### 17. Rotating Starter Step  🟠
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Starter‑step timing, but rotate the whole pattern to re‑aim the slot (useful on a crowded floor).
- **Hands:** Steady hand‑to‑hand; lead the rotation with your body while the hand stays quiet. Re‑establish the stretch at the anchor.
- **Cue:** A starter step that re‑aims the slot.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVaJrDlNB1A · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-for-beginners/

### 18. Tuck Turn Sendout  🟠
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Tuck footwork ending in a send‑out down the slot on the anchor's stretch.
- **Hands:** Tuck her (compress + turn), then use the anchor stretch to send her down the slot into the next pattern. Time the send on the "&" so she leaves on 1.
- **Cue:** Tuck, then send her out into the slot.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KKQ5wubs8eU · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-basic-patterns/

### 19. Whip with Underarm/Outside Turn  🟠
*8‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5, 6, 7&8*
- **Feet:** Whip timing.
- **Hands:** Lead an underarm (raise a clean arch) or outside turn (lead her to turn away) on 5–6, then anchor. Give the turn room; keep the anchor grounded.
- **Cue:** A whip finished with a turn — underarm or outside.
- **Video (playlist):** https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLeZ__vwnEDu8Lf_OAAuIhOHP5jK1UFBPF · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-whip/

### 20. Sugar Push Variations  🟠
*6‑count · 1, 2, 3&4, 5&6*
- **Feet:** Sugar‑push footwork — stay home.
- **Hands:** Once the basic push is solid, add syncopations or a hand change during the compression (switch hands on 3&4, or add a small tuck). Variations ride on a clean push.
- **Cue:** Dress up the push — hand changes & syncopations.
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=49apCWSUGlM · **Ref:** https://www.westcoastswingonline.com/west-coast-swing-basic-patterns/
